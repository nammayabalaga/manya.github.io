About Nammaya Balaga

Our Mission

Nammaya Balaga, which translates to "Our Group," is a cultural organization dedicated to preserving, promoting, and celebrating the rich folk traditions of our heritage. We aim to connect people of all ages and backgrounds through the vibrant arts of Yakshagaana, Bhajane, and Chende.

Our Vision

To be a leading cultural hub that inspires the next generation of artists and keeps our traditions alive through community engagement, performances, and educational workshops.
